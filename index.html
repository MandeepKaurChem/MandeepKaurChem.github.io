Welcome to Dr. Mandeep Kaur’s Research Group Website

Department of Chemistry, Guru Nanak Dev University, Amritsar, Punjab, India

We are a passionate and interdisciplinary team of researchers committed to advancing the frontiers of organometallic and lanthanide chemistry with a strong focus on both fundamental understanding and practical applications.

Our research explores:

  • Fundamental studies on organolanthanide complexes, aimed at deciphering their unique electronic structures, bonding patterns, and reactivity profiles.
  • Catalytic applications of these systems in sustainable transformations, including a detailed investigation of mechanistic cycles to gain insight into reaction pathways and intermediate species.
  • Heterometallic Catalysis: Harnessing the synergistic effects between different metals to unlock novel reactivities and enhance catalytic performance in challenging chemical reactions.
  • Medicinal applications of organometallic complexes, particularly their potential as therapeutic agents in antimicrobial and anticancer domains.

We are always looking for enthusiastic, highly motivated, and skilled students or researchers to join our growing team. If you're excited by the challenge of cutting-edge organometallic research and want to contribute, we’d love to hear from you! For inquiries or to express interest in joining the group, please contact Dr. Mandeep Kaur directly at mandeep.chem@gndu.ac.in. Don’t forget to attach your CV.
